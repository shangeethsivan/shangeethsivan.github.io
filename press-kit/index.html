Bio

Short Bio

Shangeeth Sivan is a Staff Software Engineer and speaker focused on Android, Kotlin, and AI agents. He builds scalable systems and leads developer communities through practical, builder-first education.

Long Bio

Shangeeth Sivan is a Staff Software Engineer with 9+ years of experience building Android products at scale. His work spans architecture modernization, Kotlin Multiplatform adoption, and engineering workflows powered by AI agents. He is a frequent speaker at developer events, and an active community organizer across Kotlin and Android circles in Chennai. His sessions focus on practical systems thinking: how to design reliable software, grow teams, and build with clarity in fast-moving AI ecosystems.
